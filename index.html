About Company Page


Madoc Engineering Co. is an organisation dedicated at solving the maintainance and repair welding problems of industries. It is an ISO 9001-2015 Certified company. Madoc has played a vital role in the field of maintainance welding repair and reclamation of components to enhance their service life. Madoc consumabales are now used by a number of industries like the Cement, Thermal power, Mining, Steel, Sugar and Automobile Engineering industries. We at Madoc have never looked back from the word GO, always seeking new horizons. Incorporated in the year 2017.
